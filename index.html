How I move from data to story.

This section works like a visual reporting memo: as readers scroll, the evidence board changes from data to field reporting to video production to audience strategy.

01Data

Start with the record.

Budgets, trackers, survey findings, court orders and public memos give the story a spine.

02Enterprise

Then test it outside the spreadsheet.

Enterprise reporting means finding the people, places and consequences that make the data matter.

03Video

Build for screen and sound.

As a video producer, I think in scenes, scripts, pacing, interviews and audience retention.

04Audience

Make the story findable.

SEO, metadata, headlines and structure are editorial tools when they help public-interest reporting reach readers.

About

A reporter with data habits, enterprise instincts and production range.

I started by covering city halls, election nights, neighborhoods and public systems in Indore. That work taught me that the most important stories often live between what officials say and what records reveal.

At Doordarshan and DD India, I learned how to produce for scale: scripting, segment thinking and clear public-facing storytelling. At Outlook India, I worked with digital publishing and audience analytics. At UC Berkeley, I am deepening my practice in data reporting, visualization, scraping and public records work.
